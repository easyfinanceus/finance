About the project

Finance Made Simple was created for people who need a fast, clear introduction to core US finance topics like credit scores, cards, loans, and insurance.

Our approach

  • We write in plain language without unnecessary jargon.
  • We separate educational content from advertising and opinion.
  • We update articles when market conditions or rules change.

Who this is for

New immigrants, students, and anyone learning how the US financial system works from scratch.

Important note

The content is educational and does not replace advice from a licensed professional.
