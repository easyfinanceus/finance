Frequently asked questions

How do I start credit from scratch?

Start with a secured card, keep balances low, and set up automatic payments so you never miss a due date.

Do I need a credit card if I already have a debit card?

If your goal is to build a credit score, yes, but only if you can pay the balance on time and in full.
